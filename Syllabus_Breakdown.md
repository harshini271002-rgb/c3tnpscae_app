# TNPSC AE Civil Engineering Syllabus Breakdown

Based on the official `C3 AE Syllabus Final.pdf`, here is the extracted hierarchy of the 10 Units, broken down into standardized Topics and Subtopics suitable for the Quiz Application.

## Unit I: Building Materials & Construction Practices
* **Topic 1:** Engineering materials (Properties & Tests)
  * Brick
  * Stones
  * Aggregates & M-Sand
  * Cement
  * Admixtures
  * Concrete (Self-compacting concrete)
  * Mix Design
  * Timber
  * Recycled and modern materials - glass, plastic FRP, ceramic, steel
* **Topic 2:** Construction Practices: Masonry
* **Topic 3:** Construction Equipments
* **Topic 4:** Building bye-laws
* **Topic 5:** Provisions for fire safety, lighting and ventilation
* **Topic 6:** Acoustics

## Unit II: Engineering Survey
* **Topic 1:** Basics of Surveying
* **Topic 2:** Chain Surveying
* **Topic 3:** Compass Surveying
* **Topic 4:** Plane Table Surveying
* **Topic 5:** Levelling
* **Topic 6:** Computation of area and volume: L.S. and C.S.
* **Topic 7:** Contouring
* **Topic 8:** Theodolite surveying
* **Topic 9:** Traversing
* **Topic 10:** Tacheometry
* **Topic 11:** Triangulation
* **Topic 12:** Modern Surveying Techniques

## Unit III: Engineering Mechanics & Strength of Materials
* **Topic 1:** Forces: Types & Laws
* **Topic 2:** CoG & MI
* **Topic 3:** Friction
* **Topic 4:** Stresses and Strains
* **Topic 5:** Beams: SFD & BMD
* **Topic 6:** Theory of simple bending
* **Topic 7:** Deflection of beams
* **Topic 8:** Torsion
* **Topic 9:** Combined stresses
* **Topic 10:** Stress Transformations & Failure Theories
* **Topic 11:** Analysis of plane trusses

## Unit IV: Structural Analysis
* **Topic 1:** Introduction to Analysis of Structures
* **Topic 2:** Force/Flexibility Methods of Analysis
* **Topic 3:** Displacement/Stiffness Methods of Analysis
* **Topic 4:** Arches
* **Topic 5:** Suspension cables
* **Topic 6:** Theory of columns
* **Topic 7:** Moving Loads and Influence Lines
* **Topic 8:** Matrix method
* **Topic 9:** Plastic theory
* **Topic 10:** Seismic analysis of high rise building

## Unit V: Geotechnical Engineering
* **Topic 1:** Formation & Types of soils

* **Topic 3:** Classification of Soils for Engineering Practice
* **Topic 4:** Permeability of Soil

* **Topic 10:** Pile foundation
* **Topic 11:** Soil exploration & Sampling techniques
* **Topic 12:** Compaction of Soil
* **Topic 13:** Stabilization of soil
* **Topic 14:** Ground Improvement techniques

## Unit VI: Environmental Engineering
* **Topic 1:** Sources & Demand of water

* **Topic 3:** Characteristics, analysis of water & water borne diseases
* **Topic 4:** Functional design of water treatment
* **Topic 5:** Desalination plant
* **Topic 6:** Water distribution system & Pipe network analysis
* **Topic 7:** Planning and design of sewerage system & storm water drain
* **Topic 8:** Sewer appurtenances - Pumping & plumbing system in high rise building
* **Topic 9:** Characteristics and composition of sewage
* **Topic 10:** Sewage treatment and disposal
* **Topic 11:** Industrial waste water treatment
* **Topic 12:** Solid waste management
* **Topic 13:** Air and Noise pollution control
* **Topic 14:** E-Waste management

## Unit VII: RCC / Prestressed Concrete / Steel Structures
* **Topic 1:** Design of Reinforced Concrete Structures
  * Working stress design concepts
  * Limit state design concepts
  * Design of Slabs (one way, Two way & Flat slabs)
  * Design of Beam (singly, doubly reinforced & flanged sections)
  * Design of Columns & Footings
  * Staircase & Lintel
  * Design of liquid storage structures (elevated and underground)
  * Design of Retaining wall
* **Topic 2:** Prestressed Concrete
  * Pre-stressing - systems and Methods
  * Design of Pre-Stressed Members for Flexure & Post tensioning slabs
* **Topic 3:** Steel Structures
  * Design of Bolted and Welded connections
  * Design of Tension members
  * Design of Columns and Bases
  * Design of Beams
  * Design of Plate girder and Gantry girders
  * Design of Members of Truss

## Unit VIII: Hydraulics & Water Resources
* **Topic 1:** Fluid Mechanics and Hydraulics
  * Fluid Properties (Basics)
  * Hydrostatic Pressure
  * Kinematics of flow
  * Fluid Dynamics (Applications of Bernoulli & Momentum equation)
  * Flow through Pipes (Losses)
  * Flow measurement in Channel
  * Open Channel Flow
  * Types of Pumps and Characteristics
* **Topic 2:** Water Resources
  * Water resources Planning and Management
  * Runoff Estimation
  * Hydrograph
  * Flood Routing
  * Flood Control
* **Topic 3:** Irrigation
  * Soil plant water relationship & Water requirements
  * Irrigation Methods
  * Design of Alluvial Canals
  * Design of Head works
  * Water logging and Land reclamation
  * Cross Drainage works
